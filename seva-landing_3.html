Common questions people ask us

How do I know my money is safe?
Your payment goes into a secure escrow account via our registered business account.
What if the artisan doesn't show up?
If they miss the appointment without valid reason, we remove them from our network and match you with another immediately.
Why do I have to pay a diagnostic fee?
It covers the artisan's transport and time to assess the job properly. No more "just come and see" that turns into wasted afternoons.
What happens if the job goes bad after payment?
Every job has a 14-day warranty. If anything fails within two weeks, we send the artisan back to fix it free. We don't ghost you after the job.
Do I have to download an app?
No. Everything happens on WhatsApp — the app you already use. Zero friction.
Where do you operate?
Currently Lagos Island only: Lekki-Ajah, Abraham Adesanya, VGC, and Sangotedo. We're expanding carefully to ensure quality.
How do you vet your artisans?
We verify their National ID (NIN), check past work samples, and test them on small jobs first. Only the reliable ones stay in our network.
What if I need a service you don't cover?
Message us anyway. If it's related to any home services you can think of, we likely know someone or will find one specific for the job.
